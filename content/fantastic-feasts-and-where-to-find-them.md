## Prawns Fry
The first item on my list is my mom's special prawns fry, there's nothing much to say about this other than the fact that it's ABSOLUTELY AMAZING. My mom & my grandmom have this age old recipe of just making the best prawns fry in the world & the first thing I had the second after coming home was this GENERATIONAL BANGER! 

I love her prawns so much so that if I'm ever put on death row I know exactly what I'm gonna ask for for my last meal. I've also come to the realization that I should probably study how to make this for myself or else my kids are never gonna get to experience this for themselves, but I've been postponing that...

## Quasedilla
I have no idea what a Quasedilla is, all I know is I saw my dad put it in the group chat once and I thought to myself, "That looks good and sounds Mexican, I want that!". 

I'm at home watching a movie with my dad when my mom comes back from the grocery store with a box of Quasadillas & a Korean Bun. 

Completely unprompted btw... she took it upon herself to get it for us (that's my goat). We then proceeded to demolish the Quasadilla & it was nice, I liked it...I don't remember much except being too full to have the Korean Bun.

## Brownie + Ice Cream
This was much later on into the vacation & is actually two items in the list combined into one (not brownie & ice cream). I really wanted to have brownie with ice cream and I also really wanted to go to Corner House, which is this ice cream parlor chain in Bangalore which has been around for a long time.

My friend told me it got to be famous because some actress shouted it out or something & he's salty about it cos he was sorta gatekeeping it for himself. 

Anyway, one night after dinner me and my family got in our car and drove to the nearest one and I got myself brownie with ice cream & my mom got herself this litchi thing coated in vanilla ice cream- which sucked btw, don't ever get that if you go to Corner House. Instead get a hot brownie with a scoop of vanilla ice on top, absolute masterclass of a desert.

We then proceeded to tresspass onto the basement of this apartment complex only to find out there was a Lamborghini there & lose our way out & I was losing my mind the whole time but... overall... great night.

## Kebabs & Shawarma
I honestly don't really know what a kebab is. Is it the thing you put on a skewer? that's the only reason I had it on my list, I know I've had them...but WTF ARE THEY. I asked my mom to make me chicken fry one day & she did and it was great, I really enjoyed it. 

She looks at my list much later & goes "Hey, I made u kebabs- strike it off & I looked at her like "You did? That was a kebab?". So to this day I don't know what they are but apparantly I've had them.

Because this second story is not that interesting I'm combining it with the kebab one. I wanted shawarma -> I've only ever had it once before -> Dad ordered it -> I ate it -> It was amazing.

## Shawai
I'm not sure how to spell it but it's the Arabian chicken thing. My friends in college are a huge fan of Arabian food, mostly because they are all from Kerala. I had my first Alfaham & Mandhi in college and I was mind blown the first time I ate it. 

But as a bonafied Burger enjoyer I started from enjoying it to getting annoyed by the fact that it started to become the standard birthday treat food (GIMME A BURGER GODDAMNIT!). Regardless I understand why mallu people like it so much-> it tastes good, it's a lot of food & it's fucking cheap. 

One of my friends swears by Arabian food almost like he was the one who invented the shit. He keeps mentioning Shawai is absolutely amazing every time we discuss food in college so much so that I had to add it to my list. When i went to Kerala this time I made it a thing to go out of my way to get the thing.

Me and my dad are in the middle of a hospital visit, they take my blood & say the test results will take about an hour or two & we have to have lunch anyway.

So I look at my dad and say "Dad, we gotta go to Club Shawaya. My college friends say it's the second coming of Christ".

My dad looks at me and says

"Aight bet."

So we book an auto to the place, we get there & I order a half masala shawai rice (I had called the same friend to double check our order). It shows up and IT LOOKS EXACTLY LIKE ALFAHAM MANDHI DUDE! It was the same thing except the chicken changed... it tasted almost exactly the same. I enjoyed it at first & quickly both me and my dad realized we couldn't finish it so we left half of the half shawai rice & went back to the hospital. 

fuckin arabian food.

## Thai curry
Have you ever had thai curry? No? let me break it down for you. It's like a coconut god came down and drizzled the thickest sweetest & at the same time savouriest green liquid in your mouth, the dish is absolutely amazing.

It doesn't matter where you order this from every single restaraunt has perfected this dish and it will taste just as good each time, there's nothing better than some rice along with some thai curry. But be warned it may not look like much but that's a fuckin heavy dish so make sure you have space for it.
